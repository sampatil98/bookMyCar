Reservation Policy

All reservations must be made through the BookMyCar Host website or mobile app. Bookings made through any other platform will not be honored.

Cancellation Policy

Customers can cancel their reservation up to 24 hours before the scheduled pick-up time without penalty. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled pick-up time will be charged a cancellation fee of 50% of the total rental cost. No-shows will be charged the full rental amount.

Refund Policy

Refunds will be provided for cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled pick-up time, minus the cancellation fee. No refunds will be provided for no-shows or cancellations made after the scheduled pick-up time. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the reservation.

Damage Policy

The customer is responsible for any damage to the vehicle during the rental period. The customer will be charged the full cost of repairs for any damages incurred, including but not limited to accidents, collisions, and theft.

Privacy Policy

Zoomcar Host respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. We collect only the information necessary to process your reservation and will not share your information with any third party without your consent.
